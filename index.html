About Me

I’m Trinity Klein, a solutions-driven IT professional passionate about leveraging cloud architecture, data intelligence, and user-centric design to solve real-world problems.
Graduating in May 2025 with a B.S. in Computer Information Systems from the University of Houston’s Cullen College of Engineering, I graduated cum laude. I hold the AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner certification. I combine technical expertise with creativity to design scalable, secure, and impactful solutions that solve real-world problems.
Over the last 4 years, I’ve built projects across web development, cloud architecture, database management, and application development. I’ve optimized appointment systems for a local business, automated marketing funnels, and digitized paper records.
What sets me apart is my ability to combine technical execution with strategic thinking and empathy. Whether collaborating with stakeholders or managing code, I thrive at the intersection of business, design, and technology.
